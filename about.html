About Me

My journey in the art world began while living in Botswana where I took up art in school as a subject from Cambridge University.

In India I have completed a course from JJ school of Art, Mumbai. Colour composition, nature and more recently the human figure, inspire me, all these are depicted in my works.

I have participated in several exhibitions and group shows every year since 2012.
